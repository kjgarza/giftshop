# Hero Image Prompts

Shared shop aesthetic (reuse in every prompt):

> Editorial still-life photography for a Mexican stationery and gift-wrap shop ("papelería"). Soft natural daylight, pastel palette (warm off-white paper, bubblegum pink, lilac, rose). Layered gift wrap, ribbons, hand-tied bows, paper tags. Deep plum-black accents. 16:9, 1920x1080, shallow depth of field, magazine-quality.

Negative for every prompt: no text, no logos, no watermarks, no visible faces, no brand names.
